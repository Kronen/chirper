What we do

We are a social networking and microblogging service that allow our users, called Chirpers, to read and write short messages of up to 256 characters

Chirps

You can publish 256 characters long chirps/posts. You’ll also see there is an archive of every Chirp you’ve ever made. You can see both your own Chirps and other users' Chirps.

Home or Feed

A chronological collection of Chirps made by Chirper users you're following. Catch up on the day and see what people are saying. Find interesting, timely conversations and jump in.

Direct Messages

This is your inbox for direct messages. Direct messages are 256 characters long and can be sent privately to Chirper users who are following your account. Think of them as private Chirps.

Followees

This is the list of people you are following. While you can see other users Chirps on their own profile, you will see Chirps from your followees in your home feed.

Followers

This is the list of people who follow you. While your Chirps are public for everyone to find, your followers will see your Chirps in their feed.

Likes

This shows all the Posts you have liked. You like a Chirp by clicking the small heart icon. Liking a Chirp is a great way to acknowledge another user’s message.
